<span>Share Transaction Levy (STL)</span></div><div><h3>Share Transaction Levy</h3><p>Finance Act No. 5 of 2005 was ena​cted with effect from 01.01.2005 to charge a levy on share transactions taking place in the stock exchange. The rate applicable is 0.2% on the selle​r on disposal value of the share and the 0.2% on the buyer on the purchase value of the share.
