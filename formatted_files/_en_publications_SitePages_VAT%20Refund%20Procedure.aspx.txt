<span>VAT Refund Procedure</span></div><div><h3>VAT Refund Procedure</h3><p>Registered person is entitled to claim a refund only he makes taxable supplies. But the VAT collected by the Department of Customs on the importation of goods for resale without processing will not be refunded. If any person registered under the section 22(7), any amount of VAT paid on purchases of goods or services can be claimed as a refund before commencing its commercial operation (during the project implementation period) even if there is no output.</p><p>Refund to be made a registered person may have two parts that is, it may be partly by a Credit Voucher and the balance by a cheque.</p><p>Deferment of tax takes place at the time of import. According to the provisions of the Act, those persons that have obtained this facility can make imports without paying VAT at the time of import.</p><p>A separate column has been provided in the cage showing imports in the VAT return to indicate deferred imports and a credit voucher is issued for the amount of tax so differed.</p><p>Any person who has registered under following categories can claim refunds.</p><ul><li>Exporters or Zero rated service providers under the Section 7 of the VAT Act.</li><li>A manufacturer who supplies goods(deemed exporter) manufactured by himself to a exporter </li><li>A value added service provider, who provides services to an exporter which results in improvement of the quality, character or value of the goods manufactured for export. </li><li>A project approved by the CGIR under 22(7) – (during the project implementation period).</li><li>A person engaged in any specified project.</li><li>Any supplier who supplies goods or services to a specified project or a Strategic Development Project.</li></ul><p>Further, the above persons are qualified to register as Registered Identified Persons (RIP) under SVAT Scheme and qualified to purchase goods or services without VAT.</p><p>In order to issue VAT refunds without unnecessary delays, above persons should comply with the following procedures.</p><ul><li> Duly filled VAT returns should be submitted on due dates to the correct place.
<ul><li>Returns in respects of specified projects or Strategic Development Project to be submitted to the Senior Commissioner (VAT) in 2nd floor of IRD Building.</li><li>Other returns to the DPRA unit in 7th floor. </li></ul></li><li>Guidelines issued under SEC 2008/3 and SEC 2008/04 should be followed in respect of specified projects and suppliers of those projects.</li><li> Ensure that following items are correctly stated in the returns.
<ul><li>Input VAT on imports during the period.</li><li>Input VAT on local purchases during the period.</li><li>Amount of exports during the period.</li><li>Amount of suspended sales during the period.</li></ul></li><li> Below mentioned documents should be prepared and keep with you until VAT Refund Unit
call to submit those documents.
<ul><li>a schedule of imports (deferred and upfront) with original cus-decs for the relevant period.</li><li>Export Schedule – where return exports data match with custom data or difference below 10% of declared exports.</li><li>Export reconciliation -  where return export data does not match with custom export data or difference is above 10% of declared exports.</li><li>Local purchases schedule with original tax invoices for the relevant period.</li></ul></li><li>Once above information is called by the Department, an Authorized Representative should be attended to the VAT Refund Unit for verification of above schedules and invoices.</li><li> For the process of issuing refund following requirements have to be completed.
<ul><li>Suspended sales confirmation</li><li>Place visit done by the refund officer to verify the business place.</li><li>Tax clearance certificate.</li><li>Details of correct bank account.</li></ul></li><li>If above requirements are fulfilled, refund will be generated without any adjustments.</li><li>If any adjustment in value of exports, suspended sales, input on imports and local purchases, the refund will be adjusted accordingly.</li><li>Finally, the cheque will be issued in favour of holder’s bank account r and the same time the credit voucher will be issued in the name of the registered person.​​​</li></ul></div></td></tr><tr><td name="_invisibleIfEmpty"></td></tr></tbody></table></td><td name="_invisibleIfEmpty"><div><ul><li><a>International Relations</a><ul><li><a>Double Tax Avoidance Treaties</a></li><li><a>Mutual Agreement Procedure (MAP)</a></li></ul></li><li><a>News/Notices</a></li><li><a>Guide to Taxpayers</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart</a><ul><li><a>Tax Chart - 1819</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart - 1920</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart - 2021</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart - 2122</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart - 2223</a></li><li><a>Tax Chart - 2324</a></li></ul></li><li><a>Tax Calendar</a></li><li><a>Issuing of Privilege Cards</a></li><li><a>Economic Activity Codes</a></li><li><a>Approved Charity</a></li><li><a>Employer Responsibilities (PAYE)</a></li><li><a>VAT Refund Procedure</a></li><li><a>Inactive VAT List</a></li><li><a>Latest Content Listing</a></li><li><a>Advertisements</a></li><li><a>Other Government Links</a></li><li><a>Copyright Statement</a></li><li><a>Tax Payments</a><ul><li><a>Online Tax Payment Platform (OTPP)</a></li><li><a>Document Identification Number (DIN)</a></li></ul></li><li><a>Upcoming Due Dates</a></li><li><a>Alerts And Announcements</a></li></ul></div></td></tr></tbody></table></div><div>
